About Us

Our website is a platform for students to view and give rating to their teammates. For Lecturers to easily manage their students and their groups. We provide a user-friendly interface for students to easily navigate through the website. We also provide a feature for lecturer to upload a CSV file with their course information. This feature is especially useful for lecturer who have a lot of courses to add to their schedule. We hope that our website will help lecturers and student alike manage their courses more efficiently and effectively.

Our Team
