About Us

Our website is a platform for students to view and give rating to their teammates. We provide a user-friendly interface for students to easily navigate through the website. We also provide a feature for lecturer to upload a CSV file with their course information. This feature is especially useful for lecturer who have a lot of courses to add to their schedule. We hope that our website will help lecturers and student alike manage their courses more efficiently and effectively.

Our Team

Our team consists of four members: Jax, Calvin and Eric. We are all students at the Multimedia University and we are passionate about helping other students succeed. We have worked hard to create this website and we hope that it will be a valuable resource for students and lecturer. If you have any questions or feedback, please feel free to contact us. We would love to hear from you!
